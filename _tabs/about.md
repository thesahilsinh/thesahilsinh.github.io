# 🔧 Sahilsinh Chavda

> *"Living in the Hard-Easy Effect: Where the impossible is approachable and the basic is a boss fight."*

Hi, I’m Sahilsinh Chavda — currently pursuing my Master’s in Cybersecurity at Sacred Heart University in Bridgeport, CT. I moved from India after completing my B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering (Cybersecurity specialization) at Rashtriya Raksha University.  

I love everything that runs under the hood: malware analysis, reverse engineering, digital forensics, network security, and using AI/ML to make defenses smarter. Whether it’s unpacking a binary in Ghidra, recovering deleted drone data with PyTSK, or teaching undergrads how VPNs and routing actually work — I’m all in.

## Trivia

Why cybersecurity?  
During my undergrad, the “reverse engineering and malware analysis” course completely hooked me. One of my first big wins was building a Python-based **Drone Forensics Tool (DFT)** during my research internship at DIAT (Pune). It helped army personnel and Ph.D. scholars understand encrypted drone file systems — and that moment showed me how technical skills can have real-world impact.  

Fast forward to now: I ranked **4th with a perfect 100/100** in US Cyber Quest Fall 2024 and grabbed **3rd place** in the Surakshathon CTF. Still very much a noob, but learning fast.
